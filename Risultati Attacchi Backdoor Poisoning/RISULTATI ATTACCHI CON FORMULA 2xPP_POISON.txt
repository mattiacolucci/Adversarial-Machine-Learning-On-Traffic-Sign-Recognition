Attack parameters:
- PP_POISON: percetnage of the dataset that has to be poisoned
- Trigger: pattern of pixel that represent the backdoor trigger. It will be added on the image sample in position X,Y
- X: position of the bottom right pixel of the trigger on the sample on the x-axis
- Y: position of the bottom right pixel of the trigger on the sample on the y-axis

Attack Evaluation:
- Acc Train: accuracy of the poisoned model on the poisoned training dataset
- Acc Val: accuracy of the poisoned model on the clear validation dataset
- Success Rate: number of poisoned validation samples classified as the target class over the number of total validation samples
- Attack Performance: score calculated as 4(success_rate*acc_train*(1/(2*pp_poison))*acc_val)/(success_rate+acc_train+(1/(2*pp_poison))+acc_val)
